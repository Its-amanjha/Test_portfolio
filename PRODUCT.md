# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Recruiters, hiring managers, and prospective clients looking for premium AI & Full-Stack engineering talent.

## Product Purpose
Showcase Aman's end-to-end expertise in AI, machine learning, and full-stack development through a highly interactive, memorable, and high-performance neobrutalist presentation.

## Brand Personality
* Bold
* Technical
* Interactive

Emotional goals: confidence, engineering rigor, unique creative flair.

## Anti-references
* Bland corporate templates
* Generic SaaS portfolios
* Pastel-shaded flat grids
* Muted, low-contrast typography

## Design Principles
1. **Unapologetically Bold**: Heavy black borders, offset shadows, and high-saturation neobrutalist blocks.
2. **Interactive Motion**: Micro-animations on hover and active states that respond instantly to user input.
3. **No Placeholders**: Real, production-grade text and working components rather than simulated visual templates.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
* WCAG Level AA contrast ratios on text blocks.
* Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` settings.
